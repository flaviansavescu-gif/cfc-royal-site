## Access to the CFC–Royal community
Belonging to an active community of breeders, judges and specialists who share experience, information
and best practices in the cynological field.

## Genealogical documents reserved for members
Members may request the issuance of genealogical documents reserved for them:

- Breeder's Affix;
- Certificates of Origin (Pedigree), type A;
- Certificates of Origin (Pedigree), type B.

Type C Certificates of Origin (Pedigree) may also be requested by non-members, under the conditions
provided by the World Dog Federation / Royal Federal Canine Club regulations.

## Preferential rates
Members benefit from reduced fees for:

- entering shows organised by the Club;
- attending courses, seminars and training programmes;
- products and services offered by the Royal Federal Canine Club or its partners.

## Priority within the organisation's projects
Where certain programmes, services or opportunities involve a limited number of places or selection
criteria, CFC–Royal members are given priority.

## Promotion of cynological activity
The Club promotes the results achieved by its members in competitions, the activity of registered
kennels and the projects that contribute to the development of cynology.

## Involvement in the organisation's development
Members may take part in the Club's projects, put forward proposals and contribute to the development
of the regulations, programmes and initiatives carried out under the aegis of the Royal Federal
Canine Club.

---

Membership requires the payment of an [annual fee](/en/membri/cotizatii/) of **100 RON**, set by
decision of the Board of Directors.
