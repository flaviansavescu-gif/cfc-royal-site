The Centre for the Development of Cynological Competencies is the educational and professional
structure of the **Royal Federal Canine Club / World Dog Federation Romania**, aiming to train a new
generation of specialists able to contribute to the development of a modern, balanced and credible
cynological system.

The Centre coordinates training, assessment and continuing-education programmes, with an emphasis on
competence, discipline, professional ethics and respect for the dog, the breeder and the community.

Through its projects, we support the training of:

- cynological judges;
- ring secretaries and stewards;
- event organisers;
- presenters and facilitators in the cynological field;
- as well as the development of the administrative and institutional skills needed to build a modern cynological structure.

## Judging School

The Centre's first programme is the **Judging School**, dedicated to training future judges in
accordance with **World Dog Federation** standards, through theoretical and practical modules.

### Current stage

The interviews with the **10 candidates** registered for the judge-candidate position have been
completed, and the Judging School is preparing to **begin its courses**.
