The Royal Federal Canine Club's Judging School trains future judges in accordance with **World Dog
Federation** standards, through theoretical and practical modules.

## Current stage

The interviews with the **10 candidates** registered for the judge-candidate position have been
completed, and the Judging School is preparing to **begin its courses**.

## Lecturers

The courses are taught by **World Dog Federation**-licensed judges from the Panel of Judges:
