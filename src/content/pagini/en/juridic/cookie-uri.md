## What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device, used to operate and improve the website.

## What we use

The site currently uses **only essential cookies**, needed for basic functionality.
We do not use marketing or tracking cookies without your consent.

## Analytics and third-party content

Any analytics tools or embedded third-party content (maps, video) load **only after consent**,
given through the cookie banner.

## Controlling cookies

You can manage your preferences from your browser settings and through the banner shown on
your first visit.
