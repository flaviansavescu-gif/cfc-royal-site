## Statutory leadership

- **General Assembly of Members** — the supreme governing body
- **Board of Directors** — the executive governing body

## Board of Directors

### President — Alexandru Paul Ciolac
*Founding member.* National and international representation, strategy, the relationship with the
World Dog Federation, the Genealogical Register and institutional development.

### Executive Vice-President — Iuliana Ramona Ciolac
Coordination of the Association's day-to-day executive activity, implementation of the Board's
decisions, coordination of the executive and administrative structures, oversight of the General
Secretariat and coordination of the Admission Applications Validation Committee.

### Organisational Vice-President — Florin Răzvan Martin
Planning and coordination of organisational activities, organising dog shows, competitions and
cynological events, coordination of logistics, prizes and operational support, as well as of the
territorial network of coordinators and volunteers.

### Institutional Director — Governance and Compliance — Mihail Cosmin Neagu
Institutional and organisational development, modernisation of internal regulations and procedures,
legal and institutional consultancy, monitoring of the legislative framework, development of
partnerships and implementation of good-governance, transparency and compliance standards.

### General & Administrative Secretariat — Laura Georgiana Firescu
Member records, official correspondence, administrative archive and internal organisational support.
