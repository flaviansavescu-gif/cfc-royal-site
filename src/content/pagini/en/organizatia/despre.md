## Mission

Promoting responsible breeding of purebred dogs, organising dog shows to national and
international standards, and training judges and cynology specialists.

## Vision

A transparent, scalable institutional cynological ecosystem that supports breeders, kennels
and affiliated associations.

## Values

- Rigour in applying breed standards
- Dog welfare and health
- Institutional transparency
- Support for the cynological community
