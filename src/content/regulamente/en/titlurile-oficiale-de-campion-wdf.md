__🏆 THE OFFICIAL WDF CHAMPION TITLES__

According to the regulation, WDF awards the following champion titles:

1. __Junior Champion__
2. __National Champion__
3. __International Champion__
4. __Reproducer Champion__
5. __Grand Champion__
6. __Multi Champion__
7. __World Champion__

These are __the only official WDF champion titles__.

__1. WDF JUNIOR CHAMPION__

__✔ Conditions for obtaining:__

The dog must obtain:

__➤ 4 (four) CAJC certificates (WDF)__

__➤ from at least 3 different judges__

CAJC is awarded only in the classes:

- __Very Young (9–12 months)__
- __Young (12–18 months)__

__✔ Concrete procedure:__

1. The dog is entered at shows in the Very Young and Young classes.
2. It obtains the grade __Excellent 1__ → it enters the „play-off” for CAJC.
3. The judge may award CAJC male + CAJC female.
4. When the dog accumulates __4 CAJC from at least 3 different judges__, the owner may apply for the title.
5. The WDF secretariats validate the CAJCs in the booklet.
6. WDF issues the __Junior Champion__ diploma.

__2. WDF NATIONAL CHAMPION__

__✔ Conditions for obtaining:__

The dog must obtain:

__➤ 6 (six) CAC certificates (WDF)__

__➤ from at least 4 different judges__

__➤ at least 1 CAC must be obtained at an international show__

CAC is awarded exclusively in the classes:

- Intermediate
- Open
- Working
- Winner
- Champion
- Foreign Champions

__✔ Concrete procedure:__

1. The dog participates in the eligible classes (≥ 15 months).
2. It obtains __Excellent 1__ and wins the class → it enters the play-off for CAC.
3. The judge may award __1 CAC/sex/breed__.
4. The dog must accumulate:
	- 6 CAC
	- from 4 different judges
	- including one CAC at an international show
5. When the conditions are met, the owner submits the application for the __National Champion__ title.

__✔ Important note:__

The title may be obtained __once for each year of life__ up to the age of 10.

__3. WDF INTERNATIONAL CHAMPION__

There are two possible routes.

__✔ Variants:__

__🔹 Route A (national):__

➡ __6 CACIB (WDF)__  
➡ from __4 different judges__  
➡ obtained at international shows in one’s own country

__🔹 Route B (international):__

➡ __3 CACIB (WDF)__ as follows:

- __2 CACIB__ from the __country of origin__
- __1 CACIB__ from a __WDF member country__

CACIB is awarded only:

- at international shows
- to dogs that have already received CAC
- in the adult/champion classes

__✔ Procedure:__

1. The dog is entered in the intermediate/open/working/winner/champion class.
2. It obtains CAC → it becomes eligible for CACIB.
3. The judge awards CACIB to the winner among the dogs with CAC.
4. The CACIBs are accumulated according to one of the routes.
5. The application for the __International Champion__ title is submitted.

__4. WDF REPRODUCER CHAMPION__

A title awarded on the basis of __offspring__, not on the basis of one’s own results.

__✔ Conditions:__

A dog (male or female) must have produced, from __at least 2 different litters__:

__➤ 6 different offspring with the grade EXCELLENT__

__or__

__➤ 3 offspring that have obtained the National Champion title (including Junior)__

__✔ Procedure:__

1. The evidence of the offspring’s performances is collected.
2. The pedigrees and results are verified.
3. WDF validates and issues the __Reproducer Champion__ title.

__5. WDF GRAND CHAMPION (Grand Champion)__

__✔ Conditions:__

The dog must have obtained:

__➤ 3 WDF National Champion titles__

__✔ Procedure:__

1. The dog wins National Champion in at least 3 different years of life.
2. The application is submitted to WDF with the evidence (the validated CACs).
3. WDF awards the title __only once in a lifetime__.

__6. WDF MULTI CHAMPION__

This is the supreme title in the WDF hierarchy.

__✔ Conditions:__

The dog must have obtained:

__➤ 1 Grand Champion title__

__➤ 1 International Champion title__

__✔ Procedure:__

1. Both titles are verified.
2. The application is submitted.
3. The __Multi Champion__ title is awarded __only once in a lifetime__.

__7. WDF WORLD CHAMPION__

__✔ Conditions:__

The dog obtains:

__➤ The International Certificate of Beauty__

at the __World Cup (WDF)__.

__✔ Procedure:__

1. Participation in the WDF World Cup show.
2. Obtaining CACIB at the world event.
3. WDF automatically awards the __World Champion__ title (only once in a lifetime).

__QUICK SUMMARY – The WDF titles and how they are obtained__

__Title__

__How it is obtained__

__Junior Champion__

4 CAJC from 3 judges

__National Champion__

6 CAC from 4 judges, 1 from an international show

__International Champion__

6 CACIB (national) or 3 CACIB (2 + 1 in a member country)

__Reproducer Champion__

6 offspring with EXC __or__ 3 champions

__Grand Champion__

3 National Champion titles

__Multi Champion__

Grand Champion + International Champion

__World Champion__

CACIB at the World Cup
