The __JBOB__ title represents the “__Best Junior specimen of the breed__” and grants access to __BIS Junior__.

__1. Classes eligible for JBOB__

JBOB may be attributed __exclusively__ from:

__✔ Very Young (9–12 months)__

__✔ Young (12–18 months)__

In accordance with Art. 11:

“The Very Young and Young classes will enter the play-off for the awarding of Best of Breed Junior.”

⚠ The Baby and Puppy classes do NOT take part in JBOB.

__2. Minimum conditions for competing for JBOB__

In order to enter the play-off for JBOB, the dog must:

__✔ have received the grade Excellent 1 in its class__

__✔ be the winner of the Very Young or Young class__

__✔ belong to a breed recognized by WDF__

__✔ be present at the judge’s call__

__3. The ring procedure for establishing JBOB__

The procedure is very clear:

__STAGE 1 — Individual judging of the Very Young and Young classes__

The judge:

1. assesses all the dogs, males and females, separately
2. awards grades
3. establishes the winner of each class (Excellent 1)

The following are chosen:

- __The best Very Young Male__
- __The best Young Male__
- __The best Very Young Female__
- __The best Young Female__

__STAGE 2 — Internal play-off by sex__

__➤ Among males__

The judge compares:

- the winner of the Very Young class (male)  
with
- the winner of the Young class (male)

→ The victor becomes: __JBOB Male__

__➤ Among females__

Likewise:

- the winner of the Very Young class (female)  
with
- the winner of the Young class (female)

→ The victor becomes: __JBOB Female__

__STAGE 3 — Declaration of the JBOB title__

The judge:

__✔ awards the JBOB Male title__

__✔ awards the JBOB Female title__

Note: these are __two distinct titles__, just like BOB.

__4. The right of access to BIS Junior__

In accordance with Article 11:

“…and subsequently admission to BIS Junior.”

➡ __JBOB Male and JBOB Female__ go directly into the __Best in Show Junior__ competition.

__5. The role of the judge__

The judge has full authority:

- may decide not to award JBOB if no dog is at the required level
- may withhold the title even if the dogs have the grade Excellent 1
- may stop the competition in case of irregularities (double calls, infractions)

__6. The qualification booklet requirement__

For validation:

__✔ the title is recorded in the WDF Qualification Booklet__

__✔ it is approved by the secretariat on the same day__

If the winner __does not have the booklet__, then:  
➡ JBOB is transferred to the next dog in the placement with Excellent 2 (if the local regulations allow, exactly as for CAJC).

__7. Practical summary (JBOB CHECKLIST)__

__✔ Eligible classes: Very Young & Young__

__✔ 1 male + 1 female in the play-off__

__✔ The class winners with Excellent 1 enter the comparison__

__✔ The judge compares Very Young vs. Young__

__✔ Decides JBOB Male and JBOB Female__

__✔ The title is discretionary__

__✔ Both titles go into BIS Junior__

__✔ The secretariat validates it on the same day__
