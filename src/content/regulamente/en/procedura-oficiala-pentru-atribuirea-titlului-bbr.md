*(Best Breed Representative – “The best representative of the breed”)*

The __BBR__ title is a special distinction awarded __only within the breed__, through a comparison between the main winners *of the various categories*, and it __does NOT affect access to Best in Show__.

__1. The purpose of the BBR title__

Pursuant to Art. 12:

„This title does not affect BIS, but is awarded as a comparative competition for the breed.”

Therefore:

- it is an __additional distinction__,
- a kind of internal „super-BOB” of the breed,
- awarded only at shows that opt for this title.

__2. Who may compete for BBR?__

Only __the dogs already declared winners of their category__, namely:

__✔ the winner of the Juniors (JBOB / Best Junior)__

__✔ the winner of the Adults (the best among Intermediate + Open + Working)__

__✔ the winner of the Champions (the best among Winner + Champion + Foreign Champions)__

Pursuant to Art. 12:

„BBR is awarded in a direct comparison between:  
– the best Very Young and Young,  
– the best Intermediate, Open and Working,  
– the best Winner, Champion and Foreign Champion.”

Note: "Very Young and Young" = the winner of the Juniors → JBOB.

__3. The technical procedure in the ring for determining BBR__

The procedure is simple and has a single final play-off:

__STAGE 1 — The three eligible dogs are selected__

The judge chooses __3 representatives of the breed__, already determined earlier through judging:

1. __The Juniors’ representative__ – JBOB Male or Female (depending on sex)
2. __The Adults’ representative__ – the winner of category A
3. __The Champions’ representative__ – the winner of category B

⚠ BBR is awarded separately for __males__ and __females__, exactly like BOB.

__STAGE 2 — Final three-way play-off__

The judge places the three dogs in the ring:

- the winning Junior
- the winning Adult
- the winning Champion

Then:

__✔ compares all three simultaneously__

__✔ decides which dog best represents the breed__

__✔ announces the winner JBR = Best Breed Representative__

__4. The characteristics of judging at BBR__

The title is awarded solely on the basis of:

- breed typicality,
- conformation,
- movement,
- overall balance,
- expression,
- the overall impression in relation to the WDF standard.

It is __a purely qualitative comparison__, with no requirement for scoring or additional justifications.

__5. What does NOT affect BBR__

The regulation states clearly:

__❌ BBR does NOT affect qualification for BIS__

__❌ BBR does NOT grant CAJC / CAC / CACIB certificates__

__❌ BBR does NOT change class results__

__❌ BBR is NOT required for championship titles__

__6. When is BBR awarded?__

- BBR __is not mandatory at all shows__.
- It is an option of the organizer or the judge, depending on the local regulation and the format of the show.

__7. Practical summary (BBR CHECKLIST)__

__✔ 3 dogs are chosen: JBOB, winning Adult, winning Champion__

__✔ Final play-off among the three__

__✔ The judge decides the winner per sex__

__✔ The title does not affect BIS__

__✔ It is only a breed distinction__

__✔ It is recorded in the booklet if the organizer provides for this__
