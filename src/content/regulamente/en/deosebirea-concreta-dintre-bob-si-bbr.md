__1. DEFINITION AND PURPOSE__

__BOB – Best of Breed__

→ *The best adult/champion specimen of the breed.*

BOB designates __the best male and the best female__ among:

- Intermediate
- Open
- Working
- Winner
- Champion
- Foreign Champions

__BOB is the main title for the respective breed.__

It is __mandatory__ at a WDF show.

__BBR – Best Breed Representative__

→ *The most representative specimen of the breed*  
(chosen through a comparison between Junior, Adult and Champion)

BBR is an __additional competition__, applied only if the organizer includes it.

It is a distinction of *breed typicality*, not a title required for BIS, CAC, CAJC, etc.

__BBR is not mandatory__ and does not exist at all shows.

__2. WHO COMPETES FOR THE TITLE__

__Only ADULTS and CHAMPIONS compete for BOB:__

- Intermediate
- Open
- Working
- Winner
- Champion
- Foreign Champions

Juniors do NOT compete for BOB.

__The category winners compete for BBR:__

1. __JBOB__ (the winning Junior)
2. __The winning Adult__ (from Intermediate + Open + Working)
3. __The winning Champion__ (Winner + Champion + Foreign Champions)

So BBR is a __three-way__ competition, between winners.

__3. EFFECTS AND RIGHTS OBTAINED__

__The BOB title grants:__

__✔ access to the category BIS (Best in Show)__

- BIS Adult  
or
- BIS Champions

It is an official title that __counts__ in the show path.

__The BBR title does NOT grant access to BIS__

It is clear in Art. 12:

„This title __does not affect__ BIS.”

So it does not bring:

- points for championships
- qualifications for higher stages
- access to the BIS ring

It is merely a prestige title __internal to the breed__.

__4. MANDATORY STATUS__

__BOB = mandatory__

It must be awarded at all WDF shows.

__BBR = optional__

It may be offered only:

- if the organizer includes it in the program
- if the judge considers it relevant

__5. THE MOMENT OF AWARDING__

__BOB__

It is determined during the breed judging, before the start of BIS.

__BBR__

It is awarded after the following have been determined:

- JBOB
- BOB Adult
- BOB Champions

Being a comparison between winners.

__6. THE MANDATORY PRESENCE IN BIS__

__BOB → mandatory in BIS__

The BOB winners (M + F) must go to the honor ring.

__BBR → does not take part in BIS__

Regardless of the winner.

__7. MAIN JUDGING CRITERION__

__BOB = the best ADULT/CHAMPION specimen of the breed on the respective day__

Emphasis on:

- structure
- movement
- presentation
- typicality
- overall quality

__BBR = the most representative specimen of the breed among Junior, Adult, Champion__

Emphasis on:

- typicality
- the overall appearance of the breed
- potential (includes Juniors)

BOB focuses on __current value__, BBR on __overall typicality__ (a more „theoretical” image of the breed).
