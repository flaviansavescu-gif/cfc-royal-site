__What does the „Winner” title/class mean in WDF?__

⚠ __ATTENTION:__ In WDF, the term __Winner__ is NOT a title in its own right (such as CAJC, CAC, CACIB, BOB, etc.).  
__Winner is an official competition CLASS__, and a dog becomes „Winner” when it:

➡ __is entered in the Winner class__  
➡ __meets the conditions to enter this class__  
➡ __wins that class with the grade Excellent 1__

So __„Winner” = the winner of the Winner class__, not a separate title.

__🔍 1. The conditions for a dog to be able to enter the Winner class__

According to the regulation (Art. 13 – Winner Class):

__✔ entry in the Winner class is MANDATORY for:__

- adult dogs (at least 18 months)
- __that have obtained 4 (four) CAC WDF certificates__
- __from at least 3 different judges__

These CACs must already be __validated in the qualification booklet__ before entering the show.

__🔍 2. The administrative procedure – how does the dog enter this class?__

To enter the Winner class, the owner must:

__✔ tick the Winner class at registration__

__✔ attach the proof of the CACs (the qualification booklet)__

__✔ ensure the validation of the entry by the organizers__

The organizers will verify:

- the number of CACs,
- the provenance of the judges,
- the official validation of the certificates.

If the requirements are not met → the dog is moved to another class (at the decision of the WDF delegate).

__🔍 3. The procedure in the ring – how does the dog actually become „Winner”?__

To become „Winner” (the winner of the Winner class), the dog must:

__1️⃣ enter the ring in the Winner class__

__2️⃣ be judged individually__

__3️⃣ receive the grade Excellent 1__

__4️⃣ be declared winner of the Winner class for its sex__

Only the dog with „Excellent 1” can be declared winner of the class.

__🔍 4. The rights of the dog that wins the Winner class__

The dog that wins the Winner class becomes __automatically a candidate for CAC__, and subsequently for BOB, as follows:

__✔ it takes part in the Play-off for CAC (the „champions” category):__

- Winner
- Champion
- Foreign Champions

__✔ it may win CAC → and become a candidate for the National Champion title__

__✔ it may proceed further in the comparison for BOB__

__🔍 5. Who can NOT be „Winner”?__

A dog can NOT become winner if it:

__❌ does not have 4 validated CACs before entry__

__❌ was entered in the wrong class (the delegate moves it)__

__❌ receives less than the grade Excellent 1__

__❌ is eliminated / disqualified / not judgable__

__❌ misses the class call__

__⭐ SIMPLE SUMMARY – How a dog becomes „Winner” in WDF__

__Step__

__What must happen__

1

The dog must have __4 CACs__ from __3 different judges__

2

The owner enters it in the __Winner class__

3

The organizer validates the CACs

4

The dog enters the ring in the Winner class

5

It receives __Excellent 1__ and is declared winner of the class

6

It officially becomes __Winner (the winner of the Winner class)__

__❗ IMPORTANT__

__Winner is not a separate title__ (such as CAC or BOB).  
It is __the winner of a special class__, mandatory for dogs with 4 CACs.

In WDF:

- __the title__ is CAC, CAJC, CACIB, BOB, etc.
- __Winner__ is a *placement* (the winner of the Winner class).
