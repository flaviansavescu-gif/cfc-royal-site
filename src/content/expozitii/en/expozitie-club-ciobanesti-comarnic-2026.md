A CAC dog show organised under the aegis of the World Dog Federation, held in Comarnic, Prahova County, on 4 January 2026, under the name "Străjerii Munților Cup — 1st Edition". Organiser: Străjerii Munților. Event host: Comarnic Town Hall.

**Organising team:** Mihail Cosmin Neagu (President, Străjerii Munților), Florin Răzvan Martin (CFC–Royal judge), Mihail Sorin Iacob (CFC–Royal judge), Alexandru Paul Ciolac, Iuliana Ramona Ciolac.
