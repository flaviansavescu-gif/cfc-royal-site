The Royal Federal Canine Club, represented by Alexandru Paul Ciolac and Flavian Savescu, took part in an international meeting organised by the **International Institute of Professional Cynology**, on the theme of launching a new elite programme dedicated to modernising dog shows within the **World Dog Federation**.

The meeting brought together representatives of cynological clubs and organisations from several countries, including **Kennel Club URCT (Algeria)**, **Genetic Behavior Health Work – GBHW (France)**, **Ndbu National Kennel (Nigeria)** and the **Association Cynologique Ukraine**, alongside the Royal Federal Canine Club. The proceedings were coordinated by Gabriel Djibril Kaci-chaouche (Vice-President of the WDF), Nesrine Blk (Secretary General of the IIPC) and Alexandru Paul Ciolac.

During the meeting, a clear and unified implementation structure was presented, intended for all WDF member clubs, with the aim of harmonising exhibition practices, professional standards and development directions at international level.
