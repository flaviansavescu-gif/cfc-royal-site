Mihail Cosmin Neagu, a member of the Panel of Judges of the Royal Federal Canine Club, was invited to judge at the cynological event organised by **Kynologia Polska**, under the coordination of Mrs Beata Pytlewska. The event took place on 21 June 2026 in Węgierska Górka (Poland), bringing together in a single day four canine competitions of national and international interest, including the **International Dog Show – Championship of Romania (CACIB)**.

At the international show dedicated to the Championship of Romania, the judging carried out by our representative allowed the awarding of the **Romanian Junior Champion** and **Romanian Champion** titles, recognised and issued through the Royal Federal Canine Club as an organisation affiliated to the **World Dog Federation**.

We thank the organisers, Kynologia Polska, for the trust placed in Romanian judges and for the institutional cooperation that strengthens international cynological relations between Romania and Poland.
