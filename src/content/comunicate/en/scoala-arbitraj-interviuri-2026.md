The Royal Federal Canine Club announces the completion of the **interview** stage of its **Judging School**.

The **10 candidates** who registered for the judge-candidate position have been interviewed by the
CFC–Royal team. Following this stage, the Judging School is preparing to **begin its courses**.

The training programme aims to prepare future judges in accordance with **World Dog Federation**
standards, through theoretical and practical modules.

Registration for upcoming sessions remains open through the candidacy platform:
[Submit your application](https://cfc-royal-candidaturi.netlify.app/). More details on the
[Judging school](/en/educatie/scoala-arbitraj/) page.
